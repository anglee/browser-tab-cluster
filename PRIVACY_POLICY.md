# Privacy Policy for Tab Cluster

**Version:** 1.0
**Last Updated:** February 3, 2026

## Overview

Tab Cluster is a browser extension that helps you manage your Chrome tabs and windows locally. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

## Data Collection and Usage

### What We Access

Tab Cluster accesses the following browser information to provide its functionality:

- **Tab Information**: Tab titles, URLs, and favicons to display and manage your open tabs
- **Window Information**: Window states to organize and merge windows
- **Session Data**: Recently closed tabs to enable restoration features

### What We Store

Tab Cluster stores minimal data locally on your device:

- **Theme Preference**: Your light/dark theme selection (stored in browser localStorage)
- **Hidden Tabs List**: IDs of closed tabs you've chosen to hide from the recently closed list (stored in browser localStorage; older entries are automatically removed)

### What We Do NOT Do

- **No External Transmission**: All data stays on your device. We do not send any information to external servers.
- **No User Accounts**: Tab Cluster does not require or create any user accounts.
- **No Cloud Syncing**: Your tab data is not synced to any cloud services.
- **No Analytics or Tracking**: We do not collect usage analytics or track your browsing behavior.
- **No Third-Party Sharing**: We do not share any data with third parties.

## Permissions Explained

Tab Cluster requires the following Chrome permissions:

| Permission | Why It's Needed |
|------------|-----------------|
| `tabs` | To read tab titles, URLs, favicons, and manage tab operations (move, close, activate) |
| `sessions` | To access and restore recently closed tabs |

## Data Retention

- **localStorage data** persists until you uninstall the extension or clear your browser data
- **Tab/window data** is read in real-time and never stored persistently

## Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

- **Access**: View your data directly in the extension
- **Deletion**: Uninstall the extension to remove all stored preferences
- **Portability**: Data exists only on your local device

## Children's Privacy

Tab Cluster does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13 years of age.
